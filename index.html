Commercialization of Recyclable product

This project focuses on developing a product made from recyclable materials and aims to identify the most viable market fit through a hypothesis-driven strategy. Our goal is to achieve the perfect product-market fit. To make informed recommendations, we conducted in-depth interviews, analyzed market and industry trends, and leveraged user research. This comprehensive approach guided us in exploring various markets and formulating actionable insights. Included in the project are recommendations on pricing strategy, overall success criteria, and the ideal customer persona who would benefit most from this product.

Indian Tiffin Service

This project involved developing a home delivery service for healthy, homemade Indian food, specifically designed for students seeking convenience. The aim was to create an efficient system that saves students time and effort by providing a seamless online ordering experience. The project encompassed designing and implementing a mobile application to handle orders, setting up a support program for local cooks—including providing equipment, recipes, and training—and managing the entire logistics of food preparation and delivery. Key project management skills utilized included overseeing the development and deployment of the app, coordinating with multiple s takeholders, and ensuring smooth operational workflows from order placement to delivery. The successful execution of this project highlighted expertise in managing complex systems and delivering effective solutions.
